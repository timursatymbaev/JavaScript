Flash cards application

Flash cards application is a tool that helps users to memorize and review information by displaying a question or a statement on one side of a card and the corresponding answer or explanation on the other side. The application allows users to create their own flashcards by providing a question and an answer. This application can be helpful for students and professionals looking to study and memorize information, such as vocabulary words, historical facts, or important formulas.
