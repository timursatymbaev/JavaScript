Counter application

Counter is a simple and user-friendly application that allows users to easily keep track of countable items. With a clean and intuitive interface, users can increment or decrement the count by tapping on the corresponding button. The application also allows users to reset the count to zero (by reloading page), making it easy to start counting from scratch. Whether you're counting items for inventory, tracking attendance, or simply counting for fun, counter is the perfect tool for the job.
